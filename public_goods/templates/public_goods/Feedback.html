Suppose in a group, 1 participant contributed €8, 1 participant contributed 4, and 1 participant contributed nothing. What would be the payoff of the participant who contributed €8?

Solution: €9.2

Explanation: Total contribution from all participants was 8+4+0=12. Total earnings from the project were 12*1.8=21.6. Each participant got an equal share of the earnings, that is, 21.6/3=7.2. Hence, payoff for the participant who contributed 8 points was 10-8+7.2=9.2.
