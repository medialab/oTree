General introduction to games

We will start with the tasks section of the study, which will require you to make 3 choices in total. This part of the study will allow you to earn money.

You will be grouped or paired with other participants, who are other respondents from [COUNTRY] at the beginning of each task.

The other participants in your group or pair will be different in each task: the same person will never be in your group more than once.
Your earnings in each task will depend on your decisions and those of the other participants in your group.

How will your earnings be calculated?

The other participants may not be online at this moment.
Your answers in each task will be matched with those of other participants in order to calculate your earnings from each section.

One task will be randomly selected at the end of the study. The amount of money that you will receive will correspond to your earnings in this task. The results will be shared with you at the end of the study.
