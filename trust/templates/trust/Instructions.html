Task 1

In the first task, two people participate: Person A and Person B.
Your role (A or B) will be revealed at the beginning of the task.

This other participant, with whom you will interact only once during the study, also lives in [COUNTRY].
Both participants will receive 10 Euro at the beginning of the game.

If you are Person A, you must decide how much of your 10 Euro you want to send to Person B. This amount will automatically be multiplied by 3.
If you are Person B, you have to decide, before knowing the decision of Person A, how much out of each of the amounts that Person A could send you, you want to send back.
